# Competence and Awareness (ISO 42001 7.2)

## Training Requirements

To ensure the safe and compliant operation of the Enterprise AI System, all personnel must meet the following competence requirements.

### 1. General Users
*   **Training:** "Introduction to Responsible AI Use"
*   **Content:**
    *   Understanding the AI Policy.
    *   Identifying High-Risk use cases.
    *   Data handling (PII restrictions).
    *   How to report incidents.

### 2. AI System Developers
*   **Training:** "Secure AI Development & Governance"
*   **Content:**
    *   Plugin architecture and Governance Hooks.
    *   Secure coding practices.
    *   Bias mitigation techniques.
    *   Technical documentation maintenance.

### 3. Risk Reviewers / Auditors
*   **Training:** "AI Auditing and Compliance (ISO 42001)"
*   **Content:**
    *   Interpreting Audit Logs.
    *   Conducting Risk Assessments.
    *   Regulatory reporting (EU AI Act).

## Record Keeping
Records of training attendance and certification must be maintained by HR/Compliance.

## Awareness Communication
Regular updates on AI governance will be communicated via:
*   Internal newsletters.
*   Login banners (implemented via SessionStart hook).
*   Town hall meetings.
